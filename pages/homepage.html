Raofu

Raofu is a brigading detection system for Reddit. It identifies users who have come from another subreddit to disrupt a thread.
Raofu is currently in pre-alpha, meaning only a small number of Reddit's moderators are permitted to use the tool. Raofu will eventually be open to larger scale use by moderators and researchers alike.
